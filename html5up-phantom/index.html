PEDRA is an account mockup resembling one of many Twitter users that are now Web3 enthusiast. Their interests vary from NFTs, cryptocurrency, digital clothing, memes, Vtubers, and the increasing popularity of all the metaverse related stuff. We decided to create a fake account shine a light on the irse of this types of users that are sure that this is the future of society and the internet. Through verious tweets we managed to display their fake interests just like any other similar account, with the purpose of of the account itself being a remix of all this different types of media.
